Minimal DFA from Regex

Instructions:
  • A single dot (.) means concatenation. It can be omitted in the input. For example, a.b and ab both mean a and b concatenated
  • * means star operation. For example, (ab)* means ab repeated 0 or more times
  • + means union. For example, a+b means a or b
  • Precedence in descending order: * . + (You can use parenthesis to change order of evaluation)
  • $ is used to indicate Îµ (empty string)
  • Spaces are not considered a symbol. So you can add spaces as many times as you want
